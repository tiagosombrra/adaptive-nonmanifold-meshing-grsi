Short description
-----------------
C++17 implementation of adaptive triangular meshing for non-manifold
parametric patch complexes with shared-curve compatibility. The parameter-free
GRSI script compiles the software, reproduces the five Eistute states 0–4 used
in Figure 7, exports the saved stage-4 candidate OBJ mesh, and verifies the
published 7,184-triangle target. Rejected adaptive candidates are intentionally
retained for analysis, so the stage-4 data remain available independently of
the hybrid transition-acceptance decision.
