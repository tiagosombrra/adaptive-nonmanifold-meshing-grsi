Short description
-----------------
C++17 implementation of adaptive triangular meshing for non-manifold
parametric patch complexes with shared-curve compatibility. The parameter-free
GRSI script compiles the software, reproduces the Eistute stage sequence used
in Figure 7, exports the final stage-4 OBJ mesh, and verifies the published
7,184-triangle target.
